Business Rules — Requirement 4 (source: specification 2026-07-16 · priority order)

  • Rule 1 — Coming Soon: Product not live AND verified future launch/restock date confirmed in source → Stock: Coming Soon · Visual: Upcoming · Action: Prepare assets; hold spend until launch. 0 products qualify — no verified dates found in any source.
  • Rule 2 — Out of Stock: Sellable stock = 0, no Coming Soon → 🔴 Pause Required → Pause ads; update GMC to Out of Stock; exclude from PMax listing group until restocked.
  • Rule 3 — Going to Finish: Sellable stock 1–10 (inclusive) → 🟡 Monitor → Restock alert; consider budget trim on high-spend campaigns; do not pause.
  • Rule 4 — In Stock: Sellable stock > 10 → 🟢 Healthy → No action; keep ads running normally.
  • Rule 5 — Unknown: Inventory data missing or cannot be safely joined → ⚪ Data Review Required → Verify inventory source before any advertising decision. Never treat missing stock as zero.
  • GMC Comparison: Critical Mismatch: Out of Stock + GMC "in stock". Warning Mismatch: In Stock + GMC "out of stock". No GMC data → GMC Data Unavailable (not a mismatch). Unknown stock + GMC data → Data Review Required.
