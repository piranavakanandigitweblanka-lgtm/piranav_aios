## Critical Structural Issue — Dual Blog Architecture

**Confirmed:** Posts exist under both `/blogs/news/` and `/blogs/new/` — two separate Shopify blog handles serving the same content type. This splits crawl budget, dilutes link equity, and creates potential duplicate content signals. This must be resolved before any other content strategy work proceeds.
