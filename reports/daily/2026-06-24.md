### 1. Predictive Search — Missing Products Bug Fix
- **Problem:** Customers searching (e.g. "waterproof led transformer") saw fewer products in the predictive dropdown than on the full search results page

- **Files changed:** `sections/search-predictive-grid.liquid`
- **Fix:** Removed `product.available` filter from both loops (search_suggest and search mode)
- **Output:** `predictive-search-fix-report.md`, `predictive-search-mismatch-report.md`
- **Verdict:** ✅ PASS — Dropdown now shows same products as full search page
